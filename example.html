jQuery Heroes

jQuery Heroes allows you to highlight the heroes on a page.

$('.one-hero') jQuery Heroes can highlight one hero at a time.

  • $('.two-heroes') It can also highlight
  • $('.two-heroes') two heroes at a time.

$('.one-hero, .two-heroes') You can also highlight the results of two or more selectors.

Don't sweat going from one set of highlighted heroes to another, jQuery Heroes will deal with it.
